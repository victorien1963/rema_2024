※ Please ensure the following in order to proceed with the return of goods.

  • Labels and packaging must be intact, do not cut off labels.

  • If there is a carton box when receiving the product, please place the product back into the ”original carton box” when the express delivery is picking up; do not hand the product alone to the express delivery, it will cause damage to the product’s original packaging.
  • If the product was in an express delivery bag when you received it, please place the product back to the original express delivery bag and seal for the pick-up; if the original express delivery bag is already damaged, please ask the pick-up personnel for an express delivery bag, do not hand the product alone to the express delivery, it will cause damage to the product.
  • Redemptions will be depending on the degree of damage if the product was damaged because you did not send it back according to the rules.
